Welcome to my About Me page!

Welcome once again to the official website of Diverse Annie. I am British-Cypriot neurodivergent creator born in 2007. For many years, I wanted to assist others on their journey through life; since I didn't always have the answers to the baffling questions I had about myself and my experiences. I made my dreams slowly come true by playing around with webiste builders in 2023, getting social media in 2024, and finally making my official website in 2025.

I am a passionate writer with a side of artistic flare. My written words are important to me, since they help me express and move around in the world much more efficiantly than any verbal words could. I pair my writing with art forms, such as painting, music, photography and so much more, in order to create and make sense of my world. Additionally, using and sharing my talents is what helps me survive in a world that is way different than I am; all while showing others that yes, this is me; the real me.

My Aim for this website

On this website, you will find a variety of engaing content; my most prominent feature being my blogs; where I will be sharing tips and small personal stories that can help all ages cope with their everyday challenges! Or at least, offer a bit of hope.

This is the website younger me would have needed desperately. She needed that support. She needed that ressurance. She should of not had to navigate certain experiences. But she did - and she is here. Being me. Inspiring and educating!

It is important for me to have my own world and space, where people can get to know the real me and engross themselves in it. I am way too different; but not less!
